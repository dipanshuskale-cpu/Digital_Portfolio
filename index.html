Who I Am

I am a Mechanical Engineering student at MIT Academy of Engineering (MIT-AOE), Pune, with a strong interest in robotics, automation, embedded systems, and drone technology. I enjoy exploring how mechanical systems, electronics, and software come together to solve real-world engineering problems.

What I've Built

✦ Featured Project
Smart Grass-Cutting & Water Sprinkler Robot

An Arduino-based dual-function robot designed to automate lawn maintenance. It combines a grass-cutting mechanism with an on-board water sprinkler, all controlled wirelessly from a smartphone — reducing manual effort while conserving water.

  • Wireless smartphone control via Bluetooth
  • Integrated water pump with 360° rotating sprinkler head
  • 4-wheel drive chassis built for outdoor terrain
  • Dual-function: grass cutting + water sprinkling in one unit
  • Designed for safety, comfort, and greener environments
